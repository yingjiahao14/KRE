Question: After work and errands the mother just wanted some peace and quiet, she couldn't wait to just get where? A: sea B: library C: driveway D: top of mountain E: home
Answer: Although the sea may bring happiness, the most suitable place is home. This is because after a long day of work and errands, the mother is likely to find the peace and quiet she desires in the comfort of her own home. So the answer is E: home
