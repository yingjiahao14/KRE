Question: When did the French try to create co-masonic lodges? A: the 1890s B: the 1990s C: the 1780s D: the 1900s
Answer: Continental Freemasonry is sympathetic to Freemasonry amongst women, dating from the 1890s when French lodges assisted the emergent co-masonic movement by promoting enough of their members to the 33rd degree of the Ancient. So the answer is: A: the 1890s.
