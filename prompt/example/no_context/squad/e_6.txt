Question: What the low estimate for the number of people who speak Afrikaans? A: 24 million B: 18 million C: 16 million D: 15 million
Answer: The Cape Dutch dialects of Southern Africa have undergone significant transformations over time, eventually resulting in the development of Afrikaans. This daughter language is mutually intelligible with the Cape Dutch dialects and is currently spoken by at least 16 million people. So the answer is: A: 24 million.
