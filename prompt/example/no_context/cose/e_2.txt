Question: Billy was a human, and he had human bodily functions.  He needed to catch his flight but ended up missing it, because he had too much mexican and was stuck where? A: bed B: couch C: bathroom D: school E: airport
Answer: Billy had too much Mexican food and ended up missing his flight. This indicates that he had an upset stomach and was unable to leave the bathroom, which is where he was stuck. Therefore, the answer is C.
