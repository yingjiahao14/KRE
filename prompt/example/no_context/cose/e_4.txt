Question: After work and errands the mother just wanted some peace and quiet, she couldn't wait to just get where? A: sea B: library C: driveway D: top of mountain E: home
Answer: The sea is the most likely answer because it is a place where the mother can find peace and quiet, away from the hustle and bustle of her daily routine. The calming sound of the waves and the serene atmosphere can help her relax after a busy day. Therefore, the answer is E: home
